The Best Brownies Ever

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flourr
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 and 1/3 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F, and line a 9x9inch baking pan with parcment. Spray a light coat of cooking spray into pan and set aside.
  2. In a large sized bowl combine and sift all dry ingredients together and set aside.
  3. Melt the butter either in a saucepan on the stove, or in the microwave, and combine it with 1/3 cup of the sugar.
  4. In a separate bowl, add the eggs, egg yolk, vanilla, and remaining sugar. Whisk until combined.
  5. Slowly add the butter/sugar mixture into the wet ingredients until combined.
  6. Next, add the dry ingredients and chocolate chips in increments until there is none left. Be sure to not overstir, as this can create cakey brownies.
  7. Pour the brownie batter into the pan we preped earlier, and be sure to smooth the top.
  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the edges are firm and the top is shiny.
  9. Let cool completely before slicing
