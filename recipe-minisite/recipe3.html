Strawberry Shortcake

Ingredients

  • 1 quart fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 12oz package prepared sponge cake desert cups
  • 1 can whipped cream, to taste per cake.

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl, mix the strawberries and sugar, and refrigerate until sugar is disolved. (about 15 mins)
  2. Prepare the desert cups. fill with the strawberries and top with the whipped cream
  3. For an extra sweet cup, line the bottom of the sponge cake with the whipped cream, then add the strawberries, then add more whipped cream for the topping.
  4. Optional: Garnish with the sweet strawberry juice that formed in the bottom of the strawberry bowl
  5. Serve and enjoy!
