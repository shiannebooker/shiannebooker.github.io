Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies

These cookies are a tradition in my family, and are a delicious treat that are perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup old fashioned rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F, and line a baking sheet with parcment.
  2. In a medium sized bowl beat the butter, brown and white sugar until combined.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla, mix until combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, add the flour, baking soda, and salt, and whisk until combined.
  5. Slowly add the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients bowl and mix until combined.
  6. Next, add in the chocolate chips and oats
  7. Roll into 12 balls and place them onto the baking sheet evenly spaced from eachother.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the the edges are set. They may look not quite done, but they come together perfectly once cooled.
  9. Let cool for at least 5 minutes on the baking sheet, then move to a cooling rack.
  10. Serve and enjoy!
