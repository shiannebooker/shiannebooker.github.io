This is the Rhododendron macrophyllum.

Its common names are the Pacific Rhododendron or the California Rhododendron. This plant is tecnically classified as a shrub, so its size is quite small. However, in some cases it can grow to be 15 feet tall. This plant can be found anywhere from British Columbia, CA., all the way to California, USA. Hence the name 'pacific'. This plant thrives in wet, and even snowy enviorments, and loves the forest. As the Washington state flower, it was first found west of the Cascades mountain range.
