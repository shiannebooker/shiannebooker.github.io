Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies

Makes 12 cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup old fashioned rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, beat the butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until combined.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla and mix until combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Slowly add the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients and mix until combined.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips and oats.
  7. Roll into 12 balls and place them evenly on the baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10–12 minutes, until the edges are set.
  9. Let cool for at least 5 minutes on the baking sheet, then transfer to a cooling rack.
  10. Serve and enjoy.
