Option Number 2

For this option, I am also offering an Excel sheet, but it is a little bit different and will help you stay on track with your paycheck directly, rather than having to take the entire month into account.

You'll use the same format, except now you will have 2-4 tables, depending on how often you get your paychecks. (Copy & Paste as many tables as you need.) If you get paid bi-weekly, I suggest 2 tables, and if you get paid weekly, I suggest 4. Another thing you want to take note of, Especially for your subscriptions and reoccuring bills, is the date in which they come out of your account, or the day they are due. Similar to option 1, it is advised that you find somewhere in your expenses to cut back.
