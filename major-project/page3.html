Budget Number 3

If you've made it this far into the website, you must be serious about budgeting... and tired of looking at excel sheets. So we're done with them. The third and final budget plan is a super simple, excel free budget.

Ultimately, this plan is very simmilar to the sheets, but you don't have to look at chaotic tables if it's not something you enjoy. For this plan, we're going to use the monthly pay period, but you can do paycheck by paycheck if it is easier. I hope ya'll like math because were going to be doing some today.

For our first equation, you'll need to take the amount of times you get paid, times the amount you get paid. (i.e. $700 * 2 paychecks = $1300.)

Next, subtract your NON-negotiable payments. (rent, wifi, electric bill, gas, etc)

Now, subtract your negotiable payments, things you pay by choice. (streaming service, apps/websites) While subtracting these, it's a good time to analyze if you really need them.

Finally, Look what you have left. seperate that number in half. (i.e. $100/2=$50.) You now have $50 to deposit in savings, and half to keep in your checking, or spend on yourself. Remember, Self-care products should be included in non-negotiables. (toothpaste, facewash, etc.) If you need a refresher on what expenses should go in what catagory, Click Here.

All done! Repeat this every month, paycheck, or week.
