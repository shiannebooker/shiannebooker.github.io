My Favorite Saving Tips

So I see you want to save some money? If you start implementing these tips into your daily life, you will soon have a savings account built.

  1. If you can't buy it twice, don't buy it. This is a fool proof way to make yourself realize "Maybe I dont actually need this..."
  2. If you feel the urge to buy something unnecessary, transfer the cost of it into your savings for a week. If you end up needing that money for a non-negotiable, take it out and use it. If you still want it after a week, buy it. But, if you wait a week and decide you don't need that item anymore, just keep the money in your savings!
  3. Find out if you qualify for discounts on bills like wifi and electricity.
  4. Meal Prep, or even just packing a lunch to school/work. This seems like it has nothing to do with money, but in the amount you might spend on a weeks worth of eating out, you could have preped food for 2 maybe even 3 weeks worth.
  5. Schedule at LEAST one '0 spend day' where you spend no money whatsoever
  6. If you are someone who likes a fun drink everyday, treat it as a reward, not a necessity. (i.e. you went to all of your classes and finished all assignments? Grab a fun drink on the way home.)
  7. If you're someone who needs a fun drink to start your day, go to your local Chef's Store and buy yourself the supplies to make your fav drink at home. This is a 1 time large expense, but if you utilize it, it will eventually pay off itself.
  8. Create a no *blank* jar. Find one bad habit you'd like to work on, and everytine you do that bad habit you put .50 or $1 in the jar. At the end of the month, deposit it into your savings.
  9. Unsubscribe from marketing emails. This one feels stupid, but they are literally designed to make you impulsivly buy things you probably don't need
  10. If you are not going to use it more than 1/3 of the month, you don't need it. This applys to things like accesories, electronics, etc.
  11. Stop buying on brand food. 9 times out of 10 the generic tastes the same. And honestly, I can name some brands that I actually prefer the generic.

That's all for now. This page will be regularly updated for our users to offer new and fun ways to save.
